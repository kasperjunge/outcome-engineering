# Market Transparency Trust

Consumers and market followers use Boligsiden as a clear, current, and explainable source for Danish housing-market insight before buying, selling, or interpreting market movement.

## Measures

- More users visit market-statistics and news surfaces repeatedly.
- Users move from market insight to search, saved homes, seller readiness, or agent comparison.
- Press and public discourse cite Boligsiden insights.
- Users can explain what changed in their local market without overstating certainty.

## Known / Unknown

- Known: Boligsiden publicly presents housing-market statistics and news.
- Unknown: which market-insight formats drive product actions versus passive readership.
- Working estimate: transparency is both a brand and conversion lever.
