## Who They Are

A builder — founder, PM, or engineer — who already works with coding and product agents and brings messy product thinking they want challenged before they build too long.

## Jobs / Pains

- Wants to expose hidden assumptions and find the riskiest one to learn about next.
- Drifts into building outputs without tracing them back to an outcome or vision.
- Has no durable, repo-native place to keep evolving product intent.

## Why They Choose Us

- Outcome Engineering meets them at any messy entry point and challenges product thinking in their existing agent workflow.
- The graph gives them durable product memory their agents can read and update.

## Where They Are Not A Fit

- Teams wanting full project management, roadmaps, or analytics dashboards rather than sharper product judgment.
- Users unwilling to keep product intent in a repo.
