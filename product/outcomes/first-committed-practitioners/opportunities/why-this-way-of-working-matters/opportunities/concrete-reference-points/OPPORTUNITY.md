# Solo builders need concrete reference points

Solo software product builders need concrete reference points that make Outcome Engineering feel applicable to real product decisions. Without a recognizable product context, the framework can remain an abstract taxonomy instead of a practice they can transfer to their own work.

## Evidence

- Hypothesis: Recognizable product contexts reduce the cognitive load of learning a new framework.
- Hypothesis: Users may understand the taxonomy but fail to imagine how it applies to their own product decisions.

## Known / Unknown

- Known: New users are unlikely to understand the framework from taxonomy alone.
- Unknown: Which product contexts create the most recognition and least confusion.
- Unknown: What level of realism users need before a reference point feels credible rather than merely illustrative.
