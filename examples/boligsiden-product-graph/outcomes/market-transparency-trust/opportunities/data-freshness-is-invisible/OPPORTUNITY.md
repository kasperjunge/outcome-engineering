# Data Freshness Is Invisible

```yaml
type: opportunity
id: opportunity.data-freshness-is-invisible
status: simulated
```

Users may not know when listings, price changes, sold data, or statistics were last updated, which can reduce trust in a fast-moving market.

## Evidence

- Listing and market data products depend on freshness.
- Trust issues are amplified when users act on expensive decisions.

## Known / Unknown

- Known: freshness affects confidence.
- Unknown: whether explicit freshness badges increase trust or clutter the interface.
