# Agent Choice Feels Opaque

Sellers need to choose an estate agent, but may not know how to compare local experience, fit, and likely sale strategy.

## Evidence

- Boligsiden appears to include broker/agent discovery surfaces.
- Seller decisions depend on trust, local track record, and service fit.

## Known / Unknown

- Known: comparing agents is a natural seller job.
- Unknown: which trust signals sellers recognize as fair, useful, and non-promotional.
