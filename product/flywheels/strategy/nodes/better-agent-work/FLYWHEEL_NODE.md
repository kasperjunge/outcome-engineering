# Better agent work

Agent-assisted delivery stays closer to the builder's product judgment and produces implementation, analysis, and discovery artifacts that are easier to review.

## Why this creates the next step

Better aligned work creates clearer evidence about what is valuable, risky, confusing, or strategically weak.
